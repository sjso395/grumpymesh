The First-Ever Repeater Specifically Designed for MeshCore
The Grumpy King 1 Repeater is a purpose-built repeater engineered for reliable, always-on MeshCore networks. It contains the 1 watt Grumpy Mesh Board with SMA connector and Wifi connector for easier firmware updates! This repeater is built with over 11,000 mah total battery and a 10 watt solar panel to ensure adequate power in any weather conditions! The Grumpy Repeater is designed for maximum uptime, efficient power usage, and seamless deployment in real-world environments.
